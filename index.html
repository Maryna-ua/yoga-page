Yoga is an ancient Indian practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to promote physical and mental well-being. The word "yoga" is derived from Sanskrit and means "to unite," symbolizing the union of body, mind, and spirit. Regular yoga practice can improve strength, flexibility, and balance, while also reducing stress and promoting relaxation.
